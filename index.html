Academic Writing Journey: Mastering the Scholarly Abstract

A fully asynchronous, self-paced Canva Course designed to move learners from B2 to C1 in academic English, with a minor focus on academic literacy conventions.

Course Identity

The unit is structured as a six-hour core Canva Course with 18 sequenced activities (3 per level) across six progressive levels. Learners become researchers progressing from novice explorer to Q1-publishing scholar.

Instructional Framework

SAM + Pitarch IPO + ESA + CLIL (4Cs). Progress tracking via Reporting tab • Solo Work submissions • Padlet (Level 5) • Flip/Vocaroo (Level 6).
